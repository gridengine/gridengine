Proceedings of 2nd Grid Engine Workshop

Regensburg, Germany -- September 22-24, 2003



For the second time Grid Engine users and developers from research and industry met in Regensburg.

The workshop covered again a wide variety of topics around Grid Computing in general and Grid Engine in particular.
 

  • Grid Engine 6.0 Development
  • Grid Engine Partner Developments and Applications
  • Special Interest Groups
  • ... and many more ;-) ...


Thank you all for participating and contributing to the constructive atmosphere which made this workshop again a success.

The proceedings can be found at http://gridengine.sunsource.net/workshop22-24.09.03/proceedings.html especially for those who could not join us.

You will find a list of all participants, the abstracts of the presentations and links to PDF printouts of the corresponding slides. Also included are protocols of the special interest group meetings we held on the 3rd workshop day. Please take a look.

Hope you enjoyed it again.
